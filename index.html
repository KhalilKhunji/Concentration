Concentration

Concentration is a memory-based card game in which you match pairs of the same rank and color.
You have a 6 attempts to match them correctly (only permitting 1 mistake!)
You have 10 seconds to memorize, and 30 seconds to match the pairs correctly.
Press Start Game to begin. Good luck!
